1. Fever
   ├── Cold
   │   ├── Allergy
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Influenza
   │   └── No Allergy
   │       └── Diagnosis: Common Cold
   └── No Cold
       └── Headache
           └── Diagnosis: Requires further symptoms for accurate diagnosis

2. Headache
   ├── Pain in Whole Head
   │   └── Diagnosis: Stress or Tension Headache
   └── No Cold
       ├── Pain on One Side of Head
       │   ├── Sound/Light Sensitivity
       │   │   └── Diagnosis: Migraine


3. Abdominal Pain
   ├── With Diarrhea
   │   ├── Recent Travel
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Traveler's Diarrhea or Gastroenteritis
   │   └── No Recent Travel
   │       ├── Bloody Diarrhea
   │       │   └── Diagnosis: Dysentery or Inflammatory Bowel Disease
   └── Upper Abdominal Pain
       ├── After Eating
       │   └── Diagnosis: Gastritis or Peptic Ulcer Disease



4. Cough
   ├── With Sputum
   │   ├── Fever
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Pneumonia or Bronchitis
   │   └── No Fever
   │       ├── Chronic
   │       │   └── Diagnosis: COPD or Asthma
   └── Dry Cough
       ├── With Shortness of Breath
       │   └── Recent Contact with Sick Individuals
       │       └── Diagnosis: COVID-19

5. Rash
   ├── With Itching
   │   ├── Insect Bite History
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Allergic Reaction to Insect Bites
   │   └── No Insect Bite
   │       ├── Red, Raised Patches
   │       │   └── Diagnosis: Urticaria (Hives)
   └── Without Itching
       ├── Fever
       │   ├── Joint Pain
       │   │   └── Diagnosis: Viral Exanthems or Rheumatic Fever

6. Urinary Symptoms
   ├── Painful Urination
   │   ├── Increased Frequency
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Urinary Tract Infection (UTI)
   │   └── Discharge
   │       └── Diagnosis: Sexually Transmitted Infection (STI)
   └── Blood in Urine
       └── Without Pain
           └── Diagnosis: Kidney Stones or Bladder Cancer

7. Chest Pain
   ├── Radiating to Arm
   │   ├── Shortness of Breath
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack)
   └── With Deep Breaths
       ├── Recent Long Travel
       │   └── Diagnosis: Pulmonary Embolism

8. Shortness of Breath
   ├── Sudden Onset
   │   ├── After Allergen Exposure
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Anaphylaxis
   │   └── No Allergen Exposure
   │       └── Diagnosis: Spontaneous Pneumothorax
   └── Gradual Onset
       ├── With Exercise
       │   └── Diagnosis: Asthma or Heart Failure

9. Weight Loss
   ├── With Increased Appetite
   │   ├── With Thirst
   │   │   └── Diagnosis: Diabetes Mellitus
   └── Without Increased Appetite
       ├── With Night Sweats
       │   └── Diagnosis: Tuberculosis or Cancer

10. Fatigue
    ├── With Pale Skin
    │   ├── Shortness of Breath
    │   │   └── Diagnosis: Anemia
    └── Without Pale Skin
        ├── With Swollen Glands
            └── Diagnosis: Mononucleosis or Lymphoma







Hypertension Diagnosis
├── Primary Hypertension (Essential)
│   └── No Identifiable Cause
│       └── Diagnosis: Lifestyle changes and/or medication
├── Secondary Hypertension
│   └── Caused by Another Condition
│       ├── Kidney Disease
│       │   └── Diagnosis: Treat underlying kidney issues
│       ├── Adrenal Gland Disorders
│       │   └── Diagnosis: Address specific adrenal conditions
│       └── Thyroid Problems
│           └── Diagnosis: Treat the thyroid disorder
├── Resistant Hypertension
│   └── Not Controlled by Medication
│       └── Diagnosis: Evaluate for secondary causes or complex medication strategies
└── Complicated Hypertension
    └── Associated with Organ Damage
        ├── Heart
        │   └── Diagnosis: Heart disease management
        ├── Kidneys
        │   └── Diagnosis: Chronic kidney disease management
        ├── Eyes
        │   └── Diagnosis: Retinopathy management
        └── Brain
            └── Diagnosis: Stroke prevention and management
